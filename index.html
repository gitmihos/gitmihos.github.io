The Story

My passion for finding solutions and creating tools to solve problems people encounter was first recognized when I started working for a major airline. My peers worked hard and made customers smile-- I wanted to help them smile. I learned to code, starting with HTML, CSS, and so forth, to provide tools for them. Since then, I have been fortunate to always work for a similar goal but gain various work experiences-- even a role as a Buddhist Minister. Through these experiences and close interactions with people and their lives, I found a target subject I want to tackle for the rest of my life: bringing in experiences and personal mobility. With this ultimate goal in mind, I'd like to gain skills and work with people who share similar goals of bringing joy to people's lives.
