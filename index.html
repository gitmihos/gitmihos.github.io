The Goal

My interests lie in providing moments of joy to underserved people through creating experiences, content, and solutions using the emerging XR(AR/VR/MR) technology.
I am eager to gain related skills and knowledge to accomplish this goal. I am also eagerly searching for opportunities to collaborate with people who share a similar purpose and are willing to share their expertise for the shared goal.

The Story

My passion for finding solutions and creating tools to solve problems people encounter was first recognized when I started working for a major airline. My peers worked hard and made customers smile-- I wanted to help them smile. I learned to code, starting with HTML, CSS, and so forth, to provide tools for them. Since then, I have been fortunate to always work for a similar goal but gain various work experiences-- even a role as a Buddhist Minister. Through these experiences and close interactions with people and their lives, I found a target subject I want to tackle: personal mobility. With this ultimate goal in mind, I'd like to work with people with similar goals of bringing joy to their lives and gaining skills and experiences.
